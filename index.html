Nu Rho Psi is the National Honor Society in Neuroscience, founded in 2006 by the Faculty for Undergraduate Neuroscience. Nu Rho Psi is an independent non-profit, grass-roots organization comprised of neuroscientists, like you!

Here at USC

At the University of Southern California, Nu Rho Psi serves as the premier hub for academic, professional, and fun neuroscience scholars. Nu Rho Psi systemizes students across disciplines to explore and dive into neuroscience research, internships, and pre-med outlooks.

Meet the Leadership

Dr. David McKemy

Peripheral somatosensory neurons critically differentiate innocuous stimuli from those that cause tissue damage and pain (nociception). We use a combination of cellular, genetic, and behavioral approaches to understand how these cells transduce these discrete environmental stimuli, and their contribution to inflammatory and neuropathic pain.

Dr. Dion Dickman

Synapse development, function, and plasticity using a combination of Drosophila genetics, confocal imaging, and electrophysiological approaches.
